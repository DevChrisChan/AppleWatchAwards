Apple Watch Awards Blog

Earn 33 awards in 5 hours!

Thanks for checking out this blog! You'll learn how to get 33 Apple Watch awards in 5 hours! The below list will show awards you'll earn in the following 5 hours.

7-Workout Week

Cycling Award

Elliptical Award

Functional Strength Training Award

Hiking Award

Rowing Award

Running Award

Stair Stepper Award

Swimming Award

Walking Award

Yoga Award

Core Training Award

HIIT Award

Pilates Award

Tai Chi Award

Dance Award

Cooldown Award

Each workout gives "First x Workout" and "x Workout Record". The 16 workouts available for awards are:
  • Cycling
  • Elliptical
  • Functional Strength Training
  • Hiking
  • Rowing
  • Running
  • Stair Stepper
  • Swimming
  • Walking
  • Yoga
  • Core Training
  • HIIT
  • Pilates
  • Tai Chi
  • Dance
  • Cooldown
To earn the "x Workout Record" award easily, with "Rowing" workout as an example here, all you need to do is start 5 Rowing workouts for at least 5 minutes (You could choose a time goal and set it to 5 minutes) and try not to move too much and burn too much kilocalories. On the 6th Rowing workout, set a Kilocalories goal and set it to above your highest earning Kilocalories record and tada! You've got the "Rowing Workout Record"!

Last but not least, there's the 7-Workout Week. Set any workouts with a time goal for 5 minutes (0:05) for 7 times and voila!
