Heart Month Challenge Award

To get the Heart Month Challenge Award, you have to earn 30 exercise minutes on Valentine's Day, February 14, to get the award. We found the easiest way to earn the award.

To earn the Heart Month Challenge Award the easiest way, all you have to do is to idle on an "Others" workout. Check the rings after some minutes, if the workout didn't contribute to your rings, make minor movements and it will register as an exercise minute.

Good luck on earning the award!
